Turning Traffic into Revenue
for Legal Services Company

A Legal Services Company boosted its organic growth by implementing technical SEO, targeted content, and strategic link building—resulting in 480 monthly visitors, 23 ranking keywords, and 119 backlinks, alongside an estimated 2–3x increase in conversions.

Overview

About the Company: A legal services company based in Florida, USA specializing in professional process serving, skip tracing, and affidavits of diligent search for legal documents like summons and subpoenas.
Industry: Legal Services
Project Duration: 12 Months
Main Goal: Increase organic traffic and boost sales from search

The Challenge

Struggling to generate consistent organic traffic and qualified leads. Despite having a functional website, visibility in search engines was limited, and user engagement remained low. Key performance indicators such as keyword rankings, backlinks, and traffic volume were underperforming—resulting in missed revenue opportunities.

The Solution

  1. Technical SEO & Site Optimization: I started by conducting a full technical audit of the website, identifying and fixing crawlability issues, improving page speed, and optimizing mobile performance. This ensured search engines could properly index the site while also enhancing the overall user experience.
  2. Content & Keyword Strategy: I developed a targeted keyword strategy focused on high-intent, low-competition search terms. Then, I created and optimized content around these keywords to capture relevant traffic and improve rankings for core service queries.
  3. Authority Building & Backlinks: I implemented a strategic link-building campaign, acquiring high-quality backlinks from relevant and authoritative sources. This strengthened the site’s domain authority and significantly improved its ability to rank for competitive keywords.

Tools used: SEMrush, Screaming Frog, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Google PageSpeed Insights.

The Results

  • Organic Traffic Growth: Increased to 480 monthly visitors, representing a significant lift from a previously low baseline.
  • Keyword Rankings: Expanded to 23 ranking keywords, improving search visibility across core service terms.
  • Engagement Metrics: Improved on-site engagement, with users spending more time interacting with service pages. Estimated 200–300% increase in conversions.

The Outcome

Within a short optimization cycle, this legal services company transformed its digital presence from low visibility to a steadily growing acquisition channel. Organic search became a reliable driver of both traffic and conversions, reducing dependency on paid channels and improving overall marketing ROI. The combination of technical improvements, strategic content, and authority building positioned the brand for long-term, scalable growth.
